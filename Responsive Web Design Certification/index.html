Albert Einstein

The father of relativity

Brief time line of Einstein's life

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire
  • 1880 - Moved to Munich with his parents
  • 1884 - Started his early education, attending to a catholic elementary school
  • 1893 - At 14, says he had "mastered integral and differencial calculus"
  • 1900 - His paper "Folgerungen aus den Capillaritätserscheinungen" ("Conclusions from the Capillarity Phenomena") was published in the journal Annalen der Physik.
  • 1905 - So called Einstein's annus mirabillis, which he published four papers, on the photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and the equivalence of mass and energy
  • 1940 - Became an american citizen
  • 1955 - Dies at the age of 76
